ReadBoot: Product Requirements Document
Mission
ReadBoot: ReBooting Education Through Visionary Futures. This site is dedicated to creating a visionary future for the field of education and learning.

Audience
Individuals interested in the future of education who are not afraid to explore radically different and visionary ideas.

Core Site Map (apps/web)
- Home: Visionary landing page.
- About: The "Why" behind ReadBoot.
- Contact: Engagement and collaboration.
- Imagine: Playground for radical educational theories.
- OverSee: Oversight and management tools.
- Build: Practical implementation and toolkit.
- Inspire: Gallery of visionary educational futures.

Content Pillars
- Future-focused educational data.
- Radical pedagogical frameworks.
- Visionary learning platform components.
